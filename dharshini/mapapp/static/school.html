All great Journeys began with a small step forward. Thus the trust started a co-educational matriculation school in 2004, with a vision to provide quality education that would enable a child to excel and face the global challenges with confidence, without forgetting the social responsibilities. Krishnagiri welcomed this vision and perseverance with a royal salute and extended arms to help translate the vision into a mission.
